About Me 👋

Hi, I’m Ayush, an Aspiring DevOps Engineer passionate about automation, cloud computing, and building reliable systems. My journey in DevOps has been fueled by a curiosity to understand how software can be delivered faster, safer, and more efficiently by blending development and operations together. I work extensively with Linux environments, leveraging my skills in server management and scripting to create a strong foundation for automation. My interest in the cloud has led me to gain hands-on experience with AWS services, from launching EC2 instances and configuring VPCs to managing databases with MySQL and deploying applications at scale. Along the way, I’ve built and deployed containerized applications using Docker, and taken it further with Kubernetes for orchestration and scaling. To make infrastructure reproducible and reliable, I rely on Terraform to define resources as code, ensuring consistency across environments. Automation is at the heart of what I do, and I’ve designed CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins that streamline deployments, integrate seamlessly with Git and GitHub, and accelerate delivery cycles. At the same time, I understand the importance of monitoring and reliability, which is why I use Datadog to keep systems observable and ensure high availability. For me, DevOps is not just about tools — it’s about creating a culture of collaboration, automation, and continuous improvement. I’m on a journey to keep learning, experimenting, and building real-world solutions that showcase my growth and passion for this field 🚀.
